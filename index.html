Impact of Climate Changes on Different Cultures Around the World

So, to be very sensible, climate change is not just an environmental issue, it is also a cultural one. Let us see why is it so.
Few Reasons, for this problem
  • Indigenous Knowledge: Many cultures have knowledge honed over many generations. Climate change affetcts ecosystems, making this knowledge less usable and after some time, the identity of these cultures will be forgotten
  • Livelihoods: Rising of sea levels, weather calamities, and other abnormal weather patterns affect the lives of the people
